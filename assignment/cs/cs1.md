TechSolutions is particularly focused on optimizing performance for CPU-intensive applications, high-speed data storage, and GPU-based workloads used in AI processing.

To make an informed decision, the IT team has shortlisted several benchmarking tools across various categories. The team will use these tools to assess potential hardware configurations.
